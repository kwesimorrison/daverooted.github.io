Retail management includes all the steps required to bring the customers into the store and fulfill their buying needs. Retail management makes shopping a pleasurable experience and ensures the customers leave the store with a smile. In simpler words, retail management helps customers shop without any difficulty.


Heres what retail managers do

  1. recruiting, training, and supervising staff.
  2. facilitating staff development.
  3. dealing with customer issues and complaints.
  4. overseeing pricing and inventory control.

Rules about a retail manager

  • Think About The Customer Journey
  • Support Your Product's Narrative
  • Understand Why Customers DON'T Buy
